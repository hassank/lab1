A Farm Boy

I am a Stanford undergrad, computer scientist, businessman, avid learner, caring friend, martial artist and much more.
But, at my core, I am a farm boy of Honduran and Lebanese descent.


I was lovingly raised on my family's farm in Vacaville, CA. Life on our farm instilled me with many lessons and a great education which led me to Stanford University, also fittingly known as 'The Farm' to many students.


Life on my home farm gave me a great love for nature and good food, but it was the Stanford Farm which would introduce me to my greatest passion: educational technology. I hope to one day use computer science as a vehicle to improve America's education system.
